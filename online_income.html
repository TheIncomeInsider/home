How I Found the Best Ways to Make Money as a Teen

Hi, my name’s Jake, and just a year ago, I was the typical teen looking for ways to make a little extra cash. Whether it was saving up for a new phone, buying gifts, or just wanting a little financial freedom, I had one question: “How can I make money fast?”

I started by trying the usual: babysitting, mowing lawns, and odd jobs for neighbors. It was great, but I wanted something that was more consistent and fit better with my busy school schedule. That’s when I stumbled on some amazing ideas for teens to earn money that I had never thought of before!

One of my favorite discoveries was starting my own small business. I used skills I already had, like making custom stickers and selling them online. At first, I was skeptical—would anyone actually buy my designs? But as soon as I made my first sale, I was hooked.

Here are some tips that helped me get started:
